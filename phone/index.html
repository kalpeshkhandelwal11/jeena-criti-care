About Us

  • Jeena Criticare logistics  (JCL) is the domestic healthcare arm of Jeena Group with over 117 years experience.
  • JCL is  one of the main stream logistics service providers for project based supply chain management of critical biological samples.
  • JCL is currently handling critical human blood & cord blood sample shipments for various reputed companies who operate as market leaders in Diagnostics & Stem Cell Banking sectors.
  • JCL is backed by industry experts and ethical strong management team.
  • JCL provides tailor made services within region, which includes flexibility in pickup and delivery time of shipments with dedicated OTC network.
  • JCL provides all type of Packaging for carrying shipments from one locations to another locations in various temperature requirements with Data logger facility.

Introduction

Simply speaking, we are in the business of moving precious cargo. Our principles and business practices guide us in delivering unique and reliable services to you.

Choose a responsible partner, not a vendor

We work closely with our clients to design, build and sustain a profitable and secure supply chain for them. You can be assured that JCL provides not just a great logistics service, but also a business partnership that ensures total statutory compliance and adheres to the highest standards of business ethics.

Get a solution made just for you

OUR SERVICES

Services

  • Frozen

    All temperature sensitive samples shipments

    are handled and monitored by JCL’s highly

    trained staff by proper insulation, refrigeration, special packaging (coolants, pads, absorbents in case of leakages etc.), marking hazard labels and giving express services to desired locations.

  • Air

    JCL does time sensitive deliveries in the

    domestic arena which include specialist

    chemicals, medicines, temperature controlled logistics support. We create tailor made solutions for all your air freight to give reliability to your supply chain.

     

  • Delivery

    JCL provides next day delivery of critical

    diagnostics samples. This process is enhanced by visibility to standard operating procedures, the security team, the route taken, stops taken in the route till the destination is reached

  • Reverse

    JCL provides for all logistics requirements in

    the diagnostic sector. From quick and costeffective collection, to delivery of reports back to the end users. These services helps

    to place the company ahead in the competitive space of marketing with door step reverse logistics solutions.

  • Other

    Other than our specialised services we offer

    temperature controlled warehousing and

    packaging services which includes strict

    procedures and processes at par with

    international standards which are employed for inbound, storage and outbound movement of diagnostic samples.
